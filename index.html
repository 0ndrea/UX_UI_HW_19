Designer fresh from completing UNC Charlotte's immersive UX/UI Design Bootcamp

About me. And a little more.

This intensive program equipped me with crucial skills in user research, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and more, fueling my passion for creating meaningful user experiences.

From my roots in business administration at Texas A&M University to earning a project management certification from Google, my journey led me to the dynamic realm of UX/UI design where I merge creativity and problem-solving.

My venture into UX/UI design began in networking sessions with software engineers and designers. These interactions sparked my desire to chart a path that revealed the potential for me to evolve into a great designer. From those initial conversations to intensive online research and even parting with my car to join the UX/UI bootcamp, I was determined in reshaping my professional path.
